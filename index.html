About

Remorse is a metal/hardcore band started in Evansville, Indiana in 2018. The band has played many shows all around the midwest including Ohio, Tennesse, Kentucky and more. They released their record "Giving Up In Life's Torment" in 2019, followed by the single "GFI" released in 2020. The band had planned to relase another EP, but eventually ended up dispanding before it could be released. Remorse played thier last show in their hometown of Evansville, IN on the halloween of 2021.
